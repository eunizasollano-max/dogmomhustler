My Story

I was constantly dealing with mental health challenges and relying on my family for financial assistance

I was a diligent worker who found herself lost, working in a Japanese Company, enjoying learning and working simultaneously. My dedication to every task and my commitment led to unforeseen consequences.

Symptoms of fatigue emerged and I couldn't understand what was happening, so I dismissed it as normal work stress. Forced to resign from my beloved job, it wasn't until later that I received a clear diagnosis: PTSD with secondary complications.

The weeks I spent in the hospital became a familiar second home. I fought with feelings of inadequacy and burdens, though deep down, I knew they never felt that way.

From Adversity to Triumph

April 25, 2024 became my day of courage. After five years of joblessness, I found the strength to begin anew. Despite the loud whispers of anxiety, I dared to take the first step. Now, one month later, I'm earning from the comfort of my home.

I'm finally chasing my dreams to help others, spoiling my husband and my furbaby Kuma — who is also my ESA — along the way.

Sharing my journey, I hope to warm your heart and inspire you to share your own story too and start anew. I'm here to walk beside you on your journey to success. Let's embrace it together with open arms.
