POTENTIAL CLIENTS — UBC Freebie Opt-ins
========================================

Every time someone fills out the "Grab Our Free Sneak Peek" form on ubcmain.html,
their name and email are saved automatically by Netlify.

HOW TO VIEW YOUR LEADS
-----------------------
1. Go to https://app.netlify.com
2. Log in and open your site
3. Click the "Forms" tab in the top menu
4. Select the form named "potential-clients"
5. You'll see every name + email that was submitted

HOW TO EXPORT AS A SPREADSHEET
--------------------------------
On the Forms page, click "Download CSV" (top right).
Open the file in Excel or Google Sheets — done!

NOTES
------
- Submissions appear in real time (no delay)
- Netlify free plan stores up to 100 submissions/month
