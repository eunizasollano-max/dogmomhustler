Question & Answer

What is MRR? +
MRR stands for Master Resell Rights. When you purchase UBC, you also receive the rights to resell the course and keep 100% of the profits. You don't need to create anything from scratch — just buy once and resell as many times as you want.
Who is this course for? +
UBC is for anyone who wants to build a powerful brand online — beginners, entrepreneurs, coaches, creatives, and digital marketers. Whether you're starting from zero or looking to level up your existing brand, this course has something for you.
What support do I have? +
You'll get access to a success community, weekly community calls on mindset & business, and lifetime access to any updates or new videos added to the course. You're never alone on this journey.
Do I have to resell the course? +
Absolutely not! Reselling is completely optional. You can use UBC purely for your own learning and brand-building. The MRR is simply an added bonus if you choose to use it.
Is this the same as other MRR courses? +
No. UBC was built by marketers with three decades of combined experience and covers over 22 hours of content across 22+ modules. It goes far deeper than most MRR courses on the market — covering branding, content creation, video editing, automation, and more.
Is this Network Marketing? +
No. UBC is a one-time digital product purchase — not an MLM or network marketing program. You buy the course, you learn from it, and if you choose, you resell it and keep 100% of the money. There are no tiers, no recruitment requirements, and no monthly fees.
